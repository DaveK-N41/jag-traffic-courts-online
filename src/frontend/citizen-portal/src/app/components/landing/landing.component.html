Dispute Court Myths

There are some common misconceptions around disputing tickets in court. Have you heard of any of these myths?

If I dispute a ticket, the officer won't show up to court and it will get thrown out. The court hearing will be scheduled around the officers availability to allow them to attend. Errors on the ticket will make it invalid. Errors on the ticket, such as a spelling mistake, my not be grounds for dismissal. If a ticket has points attached to it, disputing it will remove the points If a ticket is disputed, there is not guarantee points will be removed.
