Did you know?

  • Minor errors on the ticket, such as a misspelled word, may not be grounds for dismissing a ticket.
  • The officer does not need to write the speed on the ticket.
  • You do not need to sign the ticket.
  • Officers (including RCMP, municipal detachments, and Integrated Road Safety Unit members) can issue tickets across different municipalities.
  • Officer schedules are checked before setting court hearing dates.
